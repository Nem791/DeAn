    • IETLS Speaking tips - How to Use Connectors

    Connectors help in improving the quality of sentence structure while creating complex sentences.Also, it helps connect one thought to the other. Thus, giving the conversation a logical sequence.

    Useful vocabulary for expressing your opinion

    1. For stating a direct opinion

    • In my opinion...
    • From my point of view...
    • As far as I'm concerned...
    • I believe that...

    2. Vocabulary for expressing agreement:

    • I quite agree that...
    • I could not agree more...
    • You're absolutely right...
    • I am in agreement...
    • I completely agree that...
    • Yes, I agree...
    • I strongly agree that...
    • I totally agree with the given idea
    • Exactly...
    • So do I...
    • I consent that...
    • Neither do I...
    • I concur with the group who believe that...

    3. Vocabulary for expressing disagreement:

    • I strongly disagree that...
    • I disagree with the opinion that...
    • My own opinion contradicts with...
    • I totally disagree with the given idea...
    • I disapprove that...
    • On the contrary...
    • It's unjustifiable to say that...
    • However, my opinion is different from...
    • I totally do not accept the fact that...

    4. Vocabulary for expressing partial agreement:

    • To some extent...
    • More or less...
    • In a way...
    • So to speak...
    • Up to a point I agree...
    • I agree to the given statement to some extent...

    5. Vocabulary for presenting time or sequence:

    • Last/Lastly...
    • First/Firstly...
    • Second/Secondly...
    • At the same time...
    • Meanwhile...
    • Then...
    • Since...
    • When...
    • After this/that...
    • While...
    • During...
    • Following this...
    • Afterwards...
    • Simultaneously

    Useful vocabulary for each part

    1. For introduction

    • Certainly...
    • Definitely...
    • Doubtlessly...
    • Undoubttedly...
    • Without any doubt...
    • No doubt...
    • Of course...

    2. For body paragraphs when adding more information to support your opinion

    • And...
    • However...
    • Morover...
    • Futhermore...
    • In addition...
    • Also...
    • Similarly...
    • In a similar fashion...
    • Likewise...
    • Though...
    • Although...
    • As long as...
    • Even...
    • Too...
    • Besides...
    • As well as...
    • What's more...
    • Again...

    3. For conclusion

    • All in all...
    • Last/Lastly...
    • In the end...
    • To sum up...
    • To conclude...
    • In conclusion...
    • To summarize...
    • In summary...
    • Summarizing it
    • We can end it by...
    • We can conclude by...
